History of School

The schools under Dindigul Nadar Uravinmurai have long been a gift to the community, shaping the future of young minds. Established by visionary founders Shri Manickam Nadar and Shri Rathnapandian, these institutions continue to uphold their legacy of providing quality education and nurturing the leaders of tomorrow

The journey of DNU Trust began with the humble beginning of MSP School, which, though small institution, set the stage for an extraordinary path of success through immense hard work and dedication. The students of MSP School contributed immensely to this success, bringing glory to the institution and helping it grow into one of the top schools in the region. Inspired by the vision of the founders, the Dindigul Nadar Uravinmurai schools have consistently been a gift to the public, offering quality education to the younger generations. Read More…
